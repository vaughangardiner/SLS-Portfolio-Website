My Analysis of Street League Skateboarding Finals since 2010

As a passionate skateboarder I wanted to collect the data from all the SLS finals ever held and compile them into three tables which in turn could be used to join the data and perform a data analysis on them by using SQL, Pandas, Matplotlib and Tableau.
